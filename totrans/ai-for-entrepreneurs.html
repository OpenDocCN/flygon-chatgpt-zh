  • Balance:

    • Combine AI insights with human judgment.
    • Keep a manual review process for critical decisions.

C. Security and Compliance

  • Best Practices:

    • Use secure APIs and encrypt sensitive information.
    • Stay updated on industry regulations related to AI and data privacy.
