?? Step 1: Choose the Right Page Size (Kindle Format + PDF)

For Kindle & Print-ready eBooks, use:

Page Size: 6 x 9 inches (standard paperback size)

Margins: 0.75" left-right | 1" top-bottom

Font: Georgia or Times New Roman (easy to read)

Font Size: 11–12 pt (not too small)

Line Spacing: 1.5

Alignment: Justified text (clean block look)

?? Chapter 6: Publish Your eBook on Amazon Kindle (Step-by-Step Guide) 🧾🌎

You’ve written your content, designed a killer layout, and now… it’s time to go live on the biggest bookstore in the world: Amazon Kindle 💰📘

This chapter will show you exactly how to publish your eBook on Amazon KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing) — for free, even if you’re a complete beginner.

Because it gives you:

Let’s break down the process 👇

?? Step 1: Create Your Amazon KDP Account

?? Go to: https://kdp.amazon.com

1. Click “Sign up” (use your Amazon account or create one)

2. Accept terms and conditions

3. Complete your Author/Publisher info

✅ Name: Use AsphireX as your author brand

✅ Bank Account: Add your local bank details (for receiving earnings)

✅ Tax Info: Just follow the step-by-step form (India = no tax issues)

?? Tip: Use PAN card + Bank Account linked to your name (Amazon doesn’t accept UPI)

?? Step 2: Start a New Kindle eBook

From KDP Dashboard:

1. Click “Create a New Title” → Select “Kindle eBook”

2. Fill in the Kindle eBook Details:

Book Title: Use your full catchy title (e.g. Amazon Kindle Blueprint)

Subtitle: Explain the benefit (e.g. How to Earn ₹50,000+ Monthly…)

Author: AsphireX

Description: Add a compelling 500–800 word blurb

(include emojis, problem-solution-benefits, clear CTA)

Keywords: Add 7 searchable phrases (e.g. make money online, self publishing, eBooks)

Categories: Select 2 (e.g. Business > Entrepreneurship, Computers > Digital Media)

Age & Grade: Leave default unless for kids

?? Step 3: Upload Your eBook File

1. Choose: DRM - Disable it (optional, for wider reach)

2. Upload your formatted .docx or .kpf (from Kindle Create)

3. Upload your Cover File (from Canva)

Format: JPEG or PNGSize: 1600 x 2560 px

4. Optional: Upload Preview (or let Amazon auto-generate it)

?? Preview before saving! Check spacing, layout, and chapters.

?? Step 4: Set Pricing + Territories

1. Territories: Select Worldwide Rights (default)

2. Royalty Option: Choose 70% (if book is priced between $2.99–$9.99)

3. Set Pricing:

USA: $2.99 or $4.99 (start low to get sales)

India: ₹99 to ₹199 is best for viral reach

UK, Canada, Australia – Amazon auto-converts prices

?? Tip: You can change the price later. Start low → get

reviews → increase later.

?? Step 5: Hit PUBLISH 🚀

Once all steps are done:

✅ Click “Publish Your Kindle eBook”

⏳ Amazon will review it in 12–72 hours

?? You’ll get an email once it’s live

?? You get a public link to your book page (which we’ll use for promotions)

?? Bonus: Add a Paperback Version (Optional)

If you want your eBook available in physical form:

1. From Dashboard → “Create Paperback”

2. Reuse same content

3. Upload interior PDF (6x9 format)

4. Upload paperback cover (designed with spine + back cover)

5. Set price higher (₹199–₹299)

Amazon prints & ships your book on demand (no extra cost to you!) 📦📖
