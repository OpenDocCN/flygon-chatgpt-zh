INTRODUCTION

Welcome, Author!

If you've read "The ChatGPT AI Art Advantage for Authors," you know that creating compelling visuals for your book marketing doesn't have to be daunting or expensive, even if you feel graphically challenged. That book laid the foundation, showing you the incredible potential of using tools like ChatGPT 4o to bring your visual ideas to life.

Now, it's time to put that knowledge into practice! This companion workbook is your hands-on guide, taking you step-by-step through the process of creating stunning marketing visuals specifically for your books. Think of it as the practical application lab for the concepts you learned.

Workbook Goals

Our goals in this workbook are simple but powerful:

  • Build Confidence: Move from understanding AI art concepts to confidently generating your own visuals.
  • Develop Practical Skills: Master the art of prompting, refining images, and applying AI to specific marketing needs like social media graphics and cover concepts.
  • Create Tangible Assets: By the end of this workbook (and especially if you join the live challenge!), you'll have a portfolio of usable marketing visuals tailored to your book.
  • Save Time & Money: Learn to leverage AI efficiently, freeing up resources for what you do best – writing.

How to Use This Workbook

This workbook is flexible. You can work through it at your own pace, applying the exercises directly to your current book project.

It's also specifically designed to accompany the book, “ The ChatGPT AI Art Advantage for Authors ” . If you're participating in the challenge, each module corresponds to a day's focus, and the exercises will be central to our live sessions and activities. Look for the "Challenge Activity Prep" notes within the exercises!

Connecting to the Book

This workbook directly builds upon "The ChatGPT AI Art Advantage for Authors ." We'll frequently reference concepts, examples, and chapters from the book. Having the original book handy will enrich your experience, but the core exercises here are designed to be actionable on their own, assuming you grasp the fundamentals covered in the book.

Tools Needed

To make the most of this workbook, you'll need:

  1. Access to ChatGPT 4o: Ensure you have an account (free or paid) and are comfortable with the basic interface.
  2. A Web Browser: For accessing ChatGPT.
  3. This Workbook: Either the digital version (which you might be able to fill directly) or the print version (with plenty of space for your notes and ideas).

1. Starting Prompt: Use ChatGPT 4o to generate an image with the following prompt:

Generate the image and observe the result

Notes:

Refinement 1 (Adding Detail): Ask ChatGPT to modify the image, with the following prompt:

Add a sleeping cat curled up on the armchair.

Generate the image and observe the result

Notes: Did it integrate the cat naturally?

Refinement 2 (Changing Style/Mood): Now, ask, with the following prompt

Change the style to a slightly more whimsical illustration style, less photorealistic.

Generate the image and observe the result

Note - Observe the style shift. Did it retain the core elements?

Refinement 3 (Correcting Elements): Let's pretend the mug looks wrong. Ask, with the following prompt:

Make the mug on the side table a bright red color and ensure it looks like it's made of ceramic.

Generate the image and observe the result

Note: Was the specific correction accurate?
